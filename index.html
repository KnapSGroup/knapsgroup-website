about  KnapS Group

KnapS Group is a global supply chain management company that serves as an embedded operational partner for international brands in home goods, home appliances, electronics, and consumer products.

In today's dispersed manufacturing landscape, the challenge goes far beyond finding a factory. It requires orchestrating a cohesive, transparent, and efficient production network across borders. That is exactly what we do.

We don't simply connect buyers with suppliers. We embed ourselves into your operations — providing strategic oversight, rigorous quality management, and end-to-end supply chain transparency. Our team acts as your dedicated operations arm on the ground, delivering the objectivity and risk mitigation you need to scale globally with confidence.

Our founding belief is that distance should never compromise quality or reliability. This is the promise behind our mission: KnapS the World, Spark Joy — turning the complexity of global trade into your competitive advantage.

our  Services

End-to-End Supply Chain Management

Your single point of contact. We serve as your embedded command center, managing everything from sourcing to final shipment — providing full transparency at every stage.

Global Supplier Matching

We are not tied to any single region. Our global network and deep market expertise allow us to identify and vet the right manufacturing partners for your specific product, cost, and quality requirements.

Quality Assurance & Risk Management

We protect your brand with a rigorous system of factory audits, proactive quality control, and compliance verification — ensuring consistent standards across all production partners.

Value Engineering

We optimize your product design and supply chain flow to reduce your total cost of ownership, directly improving your margins without compromising quality.

Logistics & Fulfillment

From factory floor to your warehouse door. We coordinate international freight, customs clearance, and last-mile logistics to ensure on-time delivery worldwide.

Market Intelligence

Leverage our on-the-ground insights into manufacturing trends, material costs, and regulatory changes to make informed sourcing decisions ahead of the competition.
